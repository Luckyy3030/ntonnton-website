Meet Our Team

At Ntonnton Kota Shop in Polokwane, our success is built by the hardworking team behind the counter. From the founder to our chefs and assistants, each person plays a special role in serving our community with delicious kotas.

Owner & Founder

Mr. Ntonnton

The visionary behind Ntonnton Kota Shop. He started this business with a passion for creating tasty, filling meals that represent the township culture of South Africa. His leadership keeps the shop growing strong.

Our Workers

Lerato – Head Chef

Specialises in creating the perfect kota combinations. Her creativity and cooking skills keep customers coming back.

Thabo – Assistant Chef

Focuses on preparing fresh ingredients and making sure every kota is made with love and consistency.

Nomsa – Cashier

The friendly face who greets customers at the counter, takes orders, and ensures everyone is served quickly.
